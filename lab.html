My Personal Top 3 Priorities


Eating Patterns

I was a big time emotional eater my entire life and I still fall victim to it. I've had success with deconstructing cravings and rewiring the relationship between food and emotion in my brain! Food is a REALLY tough area for most people to figure out often due to patterns developed during childhood. I faced these patterns in order to affect change in my life. I continue to study the behaviors, cravings and motivations behind food.

Lowering A1C

I see the diabetes epidemic coming. I was hooked before I knew what addiction was. I struggled to keep sugar in check throughout my entire life. During my transformation, I finally made sense of sugar and how I had been eating my whole life. From here on out, I will keep my insulin response strong and maintain a healthy, realistic relationship with sugar.

Gaining Spiritual and Physical Strength

Skateboarding has provided me an outlet to stay happy and get a workout while doing so. Yoga has allowed me to heal my mind and keep my body in tip top shape to perform. These magical crafts will be a lifelong journey for me. I am pursuing mastery level in both so that I may teach others to heal their minds.
