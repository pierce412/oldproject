My Story

I am a 2019 graduate of IIN (Institute for Integrative Nutrition). A Pittsburgh native, I graduated from Carnegie Mellon University in 2007 with a degree in Information Systems. Shortly thereafter, I shipped off to serve as a V-22 Osprey Pilot in the US Marine Corps until 2017. Even as a type-A, highly successful person, I was still plagued by a slew of health problems in my late teens and early adult life due to poor nutrition, stress, and lack of spiritual purpose.

I had to figure things out. It had been calling for some time. I needed to know who I was deep down inside. It was time.

After exiting the military in 2017, my life slowly began to unwind and I ended up being hospitalized for stress.

I got the help I needed and over the next 6 months I dug in and made some lasting health changes that dramatically improved the four dimensions of my health. During this transformation, I uncovered a state of being and lifestyle that have cleared the majority of my health problems and left me with a physique that I am extremely comfortable with.

Drastic improvements to my own health, coupled with the painful experiences regarding health in my own family have inspired me to help others.

Check my transformation numbers and see my current status in the lab.

Honestly, I can tell you that the mental effects of ultra levels of happiness and increased clarity of thought outweigh the physical gains immensely. I am a calm, mature professional who is dedicated to helping you meet your health goals. Take stock and accountability in your health.
