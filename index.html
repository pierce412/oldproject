What is a health Coach?

A Health Coach is a supportive mentor who helps clients feel their best through food and lifestyle changes and personalized, one-on-one encouragement. Health coaching to me is an opportunity to transform lives by sharing my own personal experience battling mental health and addiction throughout my teens and early adult life. There is a revolution afoot in the nutrition world and I believe health coaches are a key component to battling lifestyle-related diseases.

What would my Doctor think?
